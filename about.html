About Us

Our Mission

Nyayastra exists to make legal rights accessible, affordable, and understandable for every Indian citizen.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted AI-powered legal platform — connecting citizens, lawyers, and justice.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Nyayastra was born from the frustration of seeing millions suffer due to lack of legal awareness. We’re building tools that empower people to stand for their rights — with confidence.
